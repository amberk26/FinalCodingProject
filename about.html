About Jackson's Bakery

Jackson's Bakery in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, is a locally owned bakery renowned for its custom cakes and desserts. Founded by Latonya Jackson, the bakery has been serving the community for over 20 years. Latonya is a self-taught baker who combines her natural artistic ability with a passion for desserts to create cakes that are both visually stunning and delicious. She is now moving her business online to be able to larger group of people. Recognizing the importance of expanding her reach, Latonya has embraced the digital realm. The bakery's website showcases a gallery of her creations, offers online ordering, and provides information about the mobile cupcake truck, bringing her delectable treats to various events and locations.
Beyond the allure of frosting and fondant, Latonya's journey is deeply personal. The bakery was born out of her desire to provide a brighter future for her son, aiming to fund his college education through the fruits of her labor.
